Edit Profile

Edit profile is a transformative social card game where players create fictional characters using a variety of traits. Players must use the traits associated with the character and persuade a judge on why their character is better than others. The transformative aspect helps players think more positively about certain traits they have and think about those traits more positively

Objective of game: Gain the most coins by creating characters and persuading why their traits work well together

First step

At first I started the creation of the ocean, fish and worms. Using Arraylists I spawned several fish and worms in the environment. I wanted the creatures to move naturally and have a simulated environment where creatures lived in the ocean.


Incorporating Sharks

From there I incorporated sharks into the game, having the fish run away whenever they sense a shark nearby. The shark class inherits the qualities of the fish but have a chase method to hunt down the smaller fish to eat them.
