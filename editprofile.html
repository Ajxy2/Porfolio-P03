Edit Profile

Edit profile is a transformative social card game where players create fictional characters using a variety of traits. Players must use the traits associated with the character and persuade a judge on why their character is better than others. The transformative aspect helps players think more positively about certain traits they have and think about those traits more positively

Objective of game: Gain the most coins by creating characters and persuading why their traits work well together
