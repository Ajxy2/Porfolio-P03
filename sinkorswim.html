Sink or Swim

Sink of Swim was created using Java is a hunting simulation game where you play as a submarine trying to kill the sharks trying to attack smaller fish. Its a simulated aquatic environment as the smaller fish try to eat the worms while running away from the sharks. The sharks swim around trying to survive by eating the fish, if they arenâ€™t able to eat them they will eventually die.

Work

First step

At first I started the creation of the ocean, fish and worms. I made the fish chase after the worms. I wanted the creatures to move naturally and have a simulated environment where creatures lived in the ocean.


Incorporating Sharks

From there I incorporated sharks into the game, having the fish run away whenever they sense a shark nearby. The shark class inherits the qualities of the fish but have a chase method to hunt down the smaller fish to eat them.
